About

From dinner to live music, party brunches and dancing into the early hours,
Blake has you covered whether you’re joining us for date night, a catch up with

friends or a full-fledged celebration!





Serenade



Food is served until late, from our menu which includes specials
from appetizers, side orders, grills to combos and the finest of drinks
to go perfectly.


There is a fantastic beverage selection, as well as DJs and live music
four nights a week.

More exotic features of our Serenade include; Full surround high-end
sound system,and adaptive


lighting, LED screens around the room, Fully-licenced bar with late
opening hours, Capacity of 400 persons with cabaret-style seating of
350 people.

























Bistro



Tucked away in a courtyard, is our relaxed, contemporary restaurant
and bar that is
lined with natural woods, stones, fabrics and flourishing plant life
creating a bright and
airy space; Blake Bistro remains one of Abuja’s favourite outdoor

dining destinations.
Enjoy our well-travelled food and drink over breakfast or brunch or
join us for lunch
and dinner from our all-day dining menu made up of small and

large plates designed
for sharing.
Stop by for a drink! Our Bistro incorporates a cocktail bar offering up

an enviable
selection of wines, beers, whiskeys, spirits and cocktails inspired by
our travels and
relationships with local suppliers.

…or dance the night away with DJ entertainment!








Dennis Centre



Opposite the Blake Bistro is our most impressive hall, The Dennis
Centre. A dedicated
event space offering everything from boardroom to ballroom.
Dennis Event centre is a fully flexible space that works perfectly for
conferences, food
and drinks reception, networking events or private parties such as
weddings and
birthday parties and can be used in tandem for a single multi-use
event. The fully
functional hall features surround sound bass speakers, a built in
stage and adaptable
colour change lighting.
The seating and tables can be arranged to suit different situations,
in this smart
comfortable hall


We are happy to discuss your requirements and you can be assured
we will do
everything to accommodate your needs to make sure your event
runs smoothly.




















The Management



UZOCHUKWU AKUNEDOZI


CEO, Blake Resorts.



Uzochukwu Akunedozi is an unapologetic entrepreneur and
philanthropist
extraordinaire with over 15 years of diverse experience in Construction,
Oil and gas,
hospitality and foodservice.


Uzochukwu is proud to deliver the
ultimate entertainment experience with Blake Resort’s visionary
offering. Inspired by the desire to disrupt the hospitality, food &
beverage as well as the bar & nightlife
industries, Uzochukwu has delivered on a concept that brings casual
dinners, lavish
cocktails, theatrical cabaret and exquisite live music performances to
the Nigeria
social-space.


Hospitality-focused, dedicated to excellence, and highly
knowledgeable are just a few
terms most often used to describe Uzochukwu. Having immersed
himself in the
hospitality and foodservice industry throughout his youth, Uzochukwu,
has absorbed
the importance of making people feel welcome. This gave him his
strength of character
and strong desire to create warm, inviting atmospheres where people
could relax and
enjoy themselves.


As a graduate of Economics from the University of Abuja, Uzochukwu
understands the importance of education and has used his various
scholarship schemes and donations to lift the less privileged across
Nigeria.
