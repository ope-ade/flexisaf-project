CORPORATE EVENTS



A hidden gem based at the heart of Abuja, Blake offers adaptive spaces that are
suitable for private hire packages. We can cater for corporate functions or family
celebrations – Conferences, meetings, birthdays, awards, product launches, corporate
presentations and so much more.
Our internal decor is stunning. Most of the interior is handmade by the owners
themselves from recycled wood giving it a natural finish.
We can cater for your event with a selection of menus and buffet options. We are
unique to the area, offering lots of delicious dining options, car parking and a private
location that caters for many events and family occasions.
We have hosted a variety of parties and our team is flexible, and can cater to all
requests – we specialise in creating bespoke experiences in order to make your event
an unforgettable one. So whatever the event, you can be sure you are in safe hands.
